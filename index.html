About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher of the Division of Decision and Control Systems (DCS) at KTH Royal Institute of Technology. I am fortunate to work with Prof. Karl Henrik Johansson. I finished my doctoral thesis under the supervision of Prof. Sandra Hirche, at the Chair of Information-oriented Control (ITR), Technical University of Munich (TUM). I received my B. Eng and M. Eng degrees both at Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, under the supervision of Prof. Ligang Wu.

Research

My research interests lie in

  • Value of information

  • Stochastic control

  • Risk-averse learning
