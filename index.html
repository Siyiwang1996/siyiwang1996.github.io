About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Division of Decision and Control Systems (DCS), KTH Royal Institute of Technology. I am fortunate to work with Prof. Karl Henrik Johansson. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Technical University of Munich (TUM), under the supervision of Prof. Sandra Hirche. I received my B. Eng and M. Eng degrees both at Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, under the supervision of Prof. Ligang Wu.

Research

My research interests lie in

  • Value of information for control

  • Stochastic control and optimization

  • Online learning and control
